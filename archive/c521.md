# Posture UX Audit ‚Äî C521

Suggested improvements for how people **operate and experience** Posture day to day. Each item starts from a real user moment (first launch, a notification tap, changing settings, glancing at History) and ties recommendations to that context ‚Äî not to implementation details for their own sake.

**Scope:** iOS app (onboarding ‚Üí calibration ‚Üí reminders ‚Üí check-in ‚Üí Today / History / Settings), plus Watch and lock-screen surfaces where they extend the same mental model. **No code changes** ‚Äî diagnosis and product direction only.

**Method:** Read-through of current SwiftUI flows (`App.swift`, views under `Posture/Views/`, shared services), design handoff (`docs/design-response/`), MVP audit (`docs/MVP_AUDIT.md`), and video review notes (`VIDEO_REVIEW_NOTES.md`). Where the app already fixed a known issue, it is noted briefly so this doc stays current.

---

## The habit users are building

Posture is not a ‚Äústart a session‚Äù fitness app anymore. The core loop is:

1. **Calibrate once** ‚Äî establish what ‚Äúaligned‚Äù means for this person (AirPods motion or front camera).
2. **Receive quiet nudges** ‚Äî repeating calendar reminders inside an active window.
3. **Check in in ~3 seconds** ‚Äî scan (preferred) or manual acknowledgment.
4. **Glance at Today / History** ‚Äî see alignment for the day and patterns over the week.
5. **Optionally enable Pro** ‚Äî passive Watch / AirPods monitoring and richer history.

UX work should keep that loop **low-pressure, escapable, and honest** (no fake scores, no hardware assumptions, no gamification that fights the Daylight ‚Äúobservation not command‚Äù voice).

## 1. First launch & onboarding

### 1.1 Welcome copy promises AirPods before the user chooses hardware

**When the user** opens the app for the first time and reads the welcome screen.

**What they experience:** Pillar copy says *‚Äúyour AirPods do the reading‚Äù* and *‚ÄúTap, hold still, done‚Äù* before they reach the ‚Äúdo you use AirPods?‚Äù step (`OnboardingView.swift`).

**Friction:** Camera-only users feel the app was built for someone else. They may doubt the camera path or think they chose wrong on the next screen.

**Suggested change:** Split welcome copy by path, or use neutral hardware language until after the AirPods question ‚Äî e.g. *‚ÄúA few quiet check-ins each day ‚Äî your phone or AirPods read alignment in seconds.‚Äù* Align pillar 3 with the actual ritual: notification ‚Üí choice ‚Üí scan.

---

### 1.2 Legacy installs may never see the AirPods question

**When the user** upgrades from an older build where `hasAirpods` was never stored (`GoalSettings.hasAirpods == nil`).

**What they experience:** Onboarding is skipped; `CalibrationView` defaults `hasAirpods` to `false` and opens the camera path. That may be correct, but users who *do* own AirPods never get a clear moment to opt in.

**Friction:** Silent default feels arbitrary; recalibration is the only way to ‚Äúdiscover‚Äù AirPods mode.

**Suggested change:** One-time sheet for `hasAirpods == nil`: *‚ÄúDo you use AirPods with head tracking?‚Äù* with the same two CTAs as onboarding. Persist the answer before the next calibration.

---

### 1.3 Notification permission arrives without an in-app ‚Äúwhy‚Äù screen

**When the user** finishes onboarding; `RootView` / `AirpodsRootView` call `ReminderScheduler.reschedule()`, which triggers the system notification alert.

**What they experience:** iOS permission dialog immediately after ‚Äúlet's go,‚Äù with no Posture-owned screen explaining cadence (e.g. *‚ÄúWe'll nudge you every 30 minutes between 9am and 8pm ‚Äî you can change this in Settings‚Äù*).

**Friction:** Deny rate stays high; users who deny see Settings banner later but already missed the narrative beat.

**Suggested change:** Insert a single pre-prompt screen (or modal) **before** `requestAuthorization()`, tied to the interval and active hours they will use (defaults are fine). Only then call into `NotificationService`.

---

### 1.4 Streak flame on Today conflicts with Daylight positioning

**When the user** lands on Today after calibration.

**What they experience:** A sand capsule with `flame.fill` and day count in the header (`TodayView.swift`). Design spec (`docs/design-response/design-response.md`) explicitly retires the flame in favor of quiet numerals.

**Friction:** Reads as Duolingo-style pressure ‚Äî the same concern raised in `VIDEO_REVIEW_NOTES.md` for early screens.

**Suggested change:** Replace the flame chip with a lowercase serif numeral in the toolbar area ‚Äî e.g. *‚Äú3 days‚Äù* ‚Äî or tuck streak under History. If streak freezes exist in `StreakService`, surface them only when relevant (*‚Äú1 skip left this week‚Äù*), not as a permanent gamified badge.

## 2. Calibration

### 2.1 AirPods path with buds out of ears feels like a dead end

**When the user** chose AirPods at onboarding but opens calibration without buds in.

**What they experience:** Hero state *‚Äúwaiting for AirPods‚Äù*, disabled **calibrate** button, sand pulse (`CalibrationView` airpods capture step).

**Friction:** Anxiety at first launch ‚Äî *‚ÄúIs the app broken?‚Äù* No explicit ‚Äúuse camera instead‚Äù on this screen (only on later check-ins if they had a dual baseline).

**Suggested change:** On the waiting state, offer **‚Äúuse iPhone camera instead‚Äù** that sets `hasAirpods = false` and swaps to `captureStep` without forcing app restart. Same affordance if connection drops mid-countdown (error copy exists; add escape hatch).

---

### 2.2 AirPods-only baseline locks users out of camera check-ins later

**When the user** completed AirPods-only calibration (`basePitch` stays `0`) and later wants a desk check-in without wearing buds.

**What they experience:** Check-in opens `AirpodsScanView`; `cameraScanAvailable` is false (`AcknowledgmentView` / `AirpodsScanView`), so only **check in by hand** appears ‚Äî not a camera scan.

**Friction:** User calibrated ‚Äúcorrectly‚Äù but loses the most tangible feature (3s scan) whenever AirPods aren't in. Manual check-in still counts for streak but not for Today‚Äôs alignment score ‚Äî feels like punishment.

**Suggested change:**  
- **Product:** Encourage dual capture at first AirPods calibration (brief camera baseline even for AirPods users), *or*  
- **UX:** When `cameraScanAvailable == false`, explain: *‚ÄúCamera wasn't set up ‚Äî add a 5-second camera baseline‚Äù* with one tap into `CalibrationView(.quickRecalibrate)` hybrid flow.  
- **Copy:** Make manual check-in‚Äôs effect explicit on Today (*‚Äúcounted as mindful; doesn't update alignment score‚Äù*).

---

### 2.3 Camera calibration mid-countdown abort is harsh but unexplained

**When the user** moves out of frame during the 5-second countdown.

**What they experience:** Capture stops with *‚ÄúLost your face ‚Äî let's try that again.‚Äù* (`CalibrationView.runCountdown`).

**Friction:** Correct behavior, but users don't know whether progress reset or partial data was saved.

**Suggested change:** Add one line under the error: *‚ÄúNothing was saved ‚Äî tap capture when you're centered again.‚Äù* Consider a single automatic retry before showing error (mirrors check-in scan tolerance ‚Äî see ¬ß3.3).

---

### 2.4 ‚ÄúRecalibrate‚Äù choices are engineer-facing

**When the user** opens Settings ‚Üí Recalibrate.

**What they experience:** Sheet with **quick ‚Äî 5 seconds** vs **full ‚Äî start over** (`RecalibrationOptionsView`). ‚ÄúFull‚Äù clears calibration and drops them into onboarding-style `CalibrationView` at app root.

**Friction:** ‚ÄúFull‚Äù sounds like data loss; difference from quick is unclear.

**Suggested change:** User-language labels: *‚ÄúRefresh baseline (5 sec)‚Äù* and *‚ÄúChange how you track (AirPods or camera)‚Äù* ‚Äî the second should route to hardware question + calibration, not only `hasCalibrated = false` without context.

## 3. Daily nudge ‚Üí check-in loop

### 3.1 Two doors into the same sheet ‚Äî notification vs ‚Äúcheck in now‚Äù

**When the user** taps a reminder **or** Today‚Äôs **check in now**.

**What they experience:** `AcknowledgmentView` as `fullScreenCover` ‚Äî from `App.swift` (notification) or `TodayView` (manual). Choice screen includes close (`xmark`) on all paths (fixed since earlier audits).

**Friction (remaining):**  
- Notification path clears delivered notification by index; manual path does not ‚Äî minor.  
- Foreground notifications still show banner + sound (`willPresent`) which can feel like double-prompting if they already intend to check in.

**Suggested change:** When app is active on Today, prefer a subtle in-app banner or badge on **check in now** instead of system banner for the same slot. Keep sound for true background delivery.

---

### 3.2 Choice screen: scan vs manual ‚Äî outcomes differ silently

**When the user** sees *‚Äúhow's your posture right now?‚Äù* with **scan** vs **just checking in ‚Äî manual**.

**What they experience:** Manual path records `quality: nil`, shows **noted.** result, still calls `StreakService.recordAcknowledgment`. Today alignment ring stays **‚Äî** or *‚Äúchecked in‚Äù* without a numeric score.

**Friction:** Users tap manual thinking it is ‚Äúeasier scan‚Äù; streak advances but dashboard looks empty ‚Äî *‚ÄúDid it work?‚Äù*

**Suggested change:** On the choice screen, subcopy under manual: *‚ÄúCounts for your streak; alignment score needs a quick scan.‚Äù* On done screen for manual, reinforce that message once.

---

### 3.3 Camera scan does not pause when face is lost mid-countdown

**When the user** glances away during the 3-second `QuickScanView`.

**What they experience:** Sand pill: *‚Äúbring your face back into the frame‚Äù* (good tone). Timer keeps running; samples may include gaps; if face never returns, **Couldn't see your face** state.

**Friction:** Better than old red ‚Äúnot tracking,‚Äù but timer still completes with weak data or fails at end ‚Äî feels like the app rushed them.

**Suggested change:** Pause elapsed countdown while `faceDetected == false` (resume when centered). Only fail after N consecutive seconds without face. Matches user expectation of *‚Äúhold still‚Äù* literally.

---

### 3.4 AirPods scan waiting state ‚Äî good fallbacks, one gap

**When the user** prefers AirPods but buds are in the case at check-in time.

**What they experience:** Waiting hero, **use camera scan instead** (if dual baseline), **check in by hand**, close button (`AirpodsScanView`).

**Friction:** AirPods-only calibrators still hit ¬ß2.2. Mid-scan disconnect routes to **can't hear your AirPods** ‚Äî strong copy; camera primary CTA is correct.

**Suggested change:** No major change beyond ¬ß2.2; ensure waiting copy mentions case/lid: *‚ÄúOpen the case or put them in.‚Äù*

---

### 3.5 Result language: ‚Äúresting‚Äù for poor posture

**When the user** completes a scan that detects forward collapse.

**What they experience:** Large serif **resting.** with clay tint and copy about curling forward (`AcknowledgmentView`).

**Friction:** In a wellness app, *resting* sounds positive or passive ‚Äî not ‚Äúslouched.‚Äù Some users may not connect result to action.

**Suggested change:** A/B copy in Daylight voice without alarm: *‚Äúfolded‚Äù*, *‚Äúforward‚Äù*, or keep **resting** but tighten subtitle to tie word to posture: *‚ÄúResting ‚Äî head forward. Lift the crown one inch.‚Äù*

## 4. Today ‚Äî making sense of the day

### 4.1 Alignment score vs manual check-ins

**When the user** mixes manual and scanned check-ins.

**What they experience:** `alignmentScore` averages only scored acks; subtitle *‚Äúmanual check-ins only‚Äù* when no scans (`TodayView`).

**Friction:** Good honesty, easy to miss. Users may think the app is ‚Äúnot working.‚Äù

**Suggested change:** Empty ring state CTA: *‚ÄúDo a quick scan to see today's number‚Äù* linking to `showingAck`. After first manual-only day, one-time tooltip.

---

### 4.2 Day strip hours don't match reminder settings

**When the user** set active hours 9:00‚Äì20:00 in Settings but views Today‚Äôs week card.

**What they experience:** `DayStrip` hardcodes `activeWindow: 8...20` (`DayStrip.swift`, used from `TodayView`).

**Friction:** Bars outside their actual nudge window imply reminders they never configured.

**Suggested change:** Pass `settings.activeHoursStart` / `activeHoursEnd` into `DayStrip` everywhere it renders.

---

### 4.3 ‚ÄúNext nudge ¬∑ X left‚Äù is opaque

**When the user** reads the meta row under **check in now**.

**What they experience:** *‚Äúnext nudge 3:15 pm ¬∑ 4 left‚Äù* (`TodayView` + `ReminderScheduler`).

**Friction:** ‚ÄúLeft‚Äù is easy to misread as streak or check-ins remaining, not pending notifications today.

**Suggested change:** *‚Äúnext reminder 3:15 pm ¬∑ 4 more today‚Äù* or tap-to-expand explaining how interval and active hours interact.

---

### 4.4 Passive monitoring pill without prior consent context

**When the user** is Pro with AirPods background enabled.

**What they experience:** *‚ÄúAirPods linked ¬∑ listening‚Äù* / *‚Äúwaiting for AirPods‚Äù* pill on Today (`monitoringPill`).

**Friction:** First time they see it, they may not connect to Settings toggle or orange status bar dot.

**Suggested change:** First appearance: lightweight coach mark pointing to Settings disclosure (silent tone, no recording). Tapping pill opens Settings Pro section.

---

### 4.5 Recalibration banner after 30 days ‚Äî good, could close the loop

**When the user** hasn't recalibrated in 30+ days.

**What they experience:** Lavender banner *‚ÄúTime for a quick recalibrate‚Äù* ‚Üí sheet (`TodayView`).

**Friction:** Minor ‚Äî user may not know *why* drift matters for AirPods fit.

**Suggested change:** One sentence: *‚ÄúAirPods sit differently over time; a five-second reset keeps scans fair.‚Äù*

## 5. History & reflection

### 5.1 Free users see a Pro preview that isn't actionable

**When the user** opens History without Posture+.

**What they experience:** Static `proPreviewCard` describing hour-by-hour rhythm (`HistoryView`) ‚Äî not a button.

**Friction:** Teaser without **upgrade** or **learn more** feels like dead UI.

**Suggested change:** Make the card tappable ‚Üí `PaywallView` (same as Settings postcard). Label: *‚Äúsee your rhythm ‚Üí‚Äù*.

---

### 5.2 Week narrative gate feels empty early on

**When the user** has 1‚Äì3 days of data.

**What they experience:** `HistoryNarrative` returns *‚ÄúWe need a few more days.‚Äù* until 4 distinct days with scored acks.

**Friction:** True but cold; History empty state is warmer.

**Suggested change:** Bridge copy on partial data: *‚ÄúTwo check-ins yesterday ‚Äî keep going; patterns show after four days.‚Äù* Pull counts from actual acks, not only scored.

---

### 5.3 History week strip uses wrong ‚Äúexpected‚Äù density

**When the user** uses 15-minute reminders 9‚Äì20.

**What they experience:** `expectedPerDay` in `HistoryView` is hardcoded from a 30-minute / 8‚Äì20 assumption ‚Äî not their `GoalSettings`.

**Friction:** Week strip ‚Äúfill‚Äù metaphor misrepresents how diligent they were.

**Suggested change:** Compute expected slots from live settings (same formula as `NotificationService.scheduleReminders`). Tooltip: *‚ÄúBased on your reminder schedule.‚Äù*

---

### 5.4 Journal rows use internal method names

**When the user** scans the feed under the week strip.

**What they experience:** Lines like *‚Äúaligned ¬∑ airpods‚Äù*, *‚Äúnoted ¬∑ manual‚Äù* (`rowLabel`).

**Friction:** ‚Äúairpods‚Äù and ‚Äúmanual‚Äù are implementation words.

**Suggested change:** User-facing methods: *‚Äúscan‚Äù*, *‚ÄúAirPods scan‚Äù*, *‚Äúlogged‚Äù* ‚Äî or drop method when quality word is enough.

---

### 5.5 PostureHabitsView exists but isn't in the tab model

**When the user** wants deeper education than the random tip card on Today.

**What they experience:** Tips only via `TipLine` on Today (tap to shuffle). `PostureHabitsView` is not in `MainTabView`.

**Friction:** Tips feel ephemeral; no library of habits to browse before a check-in.

**Suggested change:** Link from tip card ‚Üí `PostureHabitsView` sheet, or a fourth tab *‚ÄúTips‚Äù* if product wants more content surface ‚Äî keep low visual weight per Daylight.

## 6. Settings, Pro & trust

### 6.1 Sensitivity has no user-visible meaning

**When the user** changes Relaxed / Normal / Strict.

**What they experience:** Segmented control only (`SettingsView`) ‚Äî affects `PostureScoring` thresholds.

**Friction:** Users blame ‚Äúwrong‚Äù scan results on hardware, not sensitivity.

**Suggested change:** Footnote under picker: *‚ÄúRelaxed forgives small forward head tilt; Strict flags it sooner.‚Äù* Optional live example on recalibrate screen.

---

### 6.2 Pro value is split across phone and watch

**When the user** toggles **Always-on Watch monitoring** on iPhone.

**What they experience:** Alert *‚ÄúOpen Posture on your Watch‚Ä¶‚Äù* (`SettingsView`) ‚Äî improved. Watch toggle still only fully applies when watch app runs (`WatchSettingsView`).

**Friction:** Users expect immediate haptics; slouch count on watch resets on relaunch (audit note on `totalSlouchEvents`).

**Suggested change:** On iPhone, show watch connection state (reachable / not installed). On watch, persist daily slouch count in App Group. Copy: *‚ÄúLast synced 2 min ago‚Äù* when `WCSession` is active.

---

### 6.3 AirPods background + orange indicator ‚Äî disclosure is good; first enable needs timing

**When the user** enables **Quiet AirPods background**.

**What they experience:** Caption explains orange dot and silent tone (`SettingsView`). Toggle may start monitor before they read caption.

**Suggested change:** On first enable, modal with the same copy **before** `airpodsBackgroundEnabled` flips true. Require explicit **Turn on** ‚Äî reduces surprise and App Review risk.

---

### 6.4 No way to change AirPods vs camera without ‚Äúfull‚Äù recalibrate

**When the user** bought AirPods after starting on camera (or vice versa).

**What they experience:** Must use Recalibrate ‚Üí full, or hack `hasAirpods` via hidden paths.

**Suggested change:** Settings row: *‚ÄúHow you check in‚Äù* ‚Üí AirPods / Camera / Both, writes `hasAirpods` and launches appropriate quick calibration.

## 7. Watch, widgets & passive surfaces

### 7.1 Lock screen widget still says ‚ÄúNo session today‚Äù

**When the user** added the lock screen widget but only uses acknowledgment scans.

**What they experience:** Widget reads `AcknowledgmentRecord` (fixed data model) but empty copy still says **No session today** (`PostureLockScreenWidgetView`).

**Friction:** Legacy session language; streak shown instead of score in circular variant can confuse.

**Suggested change:** *‚ÄúNo check-in yet today‚Äù* / *‚ÄúTap to check in‚Äù* deep link if possible. Widget display name still **Posture Streak** ‚Äî consider **Posture today**.

---

### 7.2 Watch app is read-mostly

**When the user** opens Posture on Apple Watch.

**What they experience:** Streak, ring from best today score, *‚ÄúCheck in on your iPhone‚Äù* (`WatchTodayView`). No watch-native acknowledgment.

**Friction:** Wrist is natural for reminder follow-through; phone requirement adds friction.

**Suggested change (phased):** Watch complication / app: **Log check-in** (manual) syncs via App Group; optional haptic-confirmed ‚ÄúI reset posture‚Äù for Pro. Keep 3s scan on phone.

---

### 7.3 Passive timeline (Pro) ‚Äî powerful but invisible until data exists

**When the user** subscribes and expects ‚Äúslouch hours.‚Äù

**What they experience:** `PassiveTimelineView` only when samples exist; empty *‚ÄúNo slouches yet ‚Äî steady so far.‚Äù*

**Friction:** Users don't know they must enable Watch always-on or AirPods background first.

**Suggested change:** Empty state checklist: Watch toggle on + watch app opened; or AirPods background on + buds in. Link each line to Settings.

## 8. Paywall & upgrade moments

### 8.1 Paywall loading is handled; offline still shows static pricing

**When the user** opens paywall on slow or offline network.

**What they experience:** *‚Äúloading plans‚Ä¶‚Äù* then RevenueCat UI or placeholder after timeout (`PaywallView`) ‚Äî good. Placeholder shows hardcoded *$29.99/yr*.

**Friction:** Price mismatch if offering changes ‚Äî trust issue at purchase tap.

**Suggested change:** Placeholder CTA: *‚ÄúConnect to load plans‚Äù* without numeric price until offerings load; disable purchase until live packages.

---

### 8.2 Upgrade only obvious from Settings

**When the user** hits free History limits or wants passive monitoring.

**What they experience:** Paywall from Settings postcard only (no paywall from History preview ‚Äî ¬ß5.1).

**Suggested change:** Consistent **Posture+** entry on History preview, passive empty state, and after N days of streak (soft, not blocking).

## Already in good shape (since earlier audits)

These were real user blockers; the current codebase addresses them. Keep them when changing adjacent code:

- **AirPods vs camera** question at end of onboarding.
- **Close** on acknowledgment choice, scan, and done screens.
- **Camera permission denied** and **no face** paths in `QuickScanView` ‚Äî no fabricated ‚Äúgood‚Äù scan.
- **Supportive** face pill copy (sand, not red alarm).
- **Camera fallback** on AirPods check-in when dual baseline exists.
- **Repeating daily** notification triggers (reminders survive without app open).
- **Notification denied** banner in Settings without silently flipping toggle off.
- **Watch sync** alert when enabling always-on from iPhone.
- **Orange dot** explanation for AirPods background audio.
- **Calibration** hint when capture disabled: *‚Äúalign your face inside the guide‚Ä¶‚Äù*
