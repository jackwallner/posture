Sit up straight — and actually stick with it.

Posture is a gentle, on-device posture coach for iPhone, AirPods, and Apple Watch. Rather than nagging you all day, it nudges you a few times a day to take a quick posture check. Keep a good streak going and watch your habit grow.

HOW IT WORKS
• Calibrate once. Sit the way you want to sit, and your AirPods learn your personal baseline in a few seconds.
• Pick your rhythm. Choose how many reminders you'd like each day and the hours you want them to land.
• Tap a reminder. A 3-second AirPods check reads your head position and tells you whether you're upright, drifting, or slouching — or just tell us how you're sitting by hand.
• Build the streak. Every day you respond to your reminders, your flame grows. Freeze protection covers the off days.

THE FREE TIER
• Quick 3-second posture checks using your AirPods' motion sensor
• Manual check-ins for when your AirPods aren't in
• Personal calibration baseline
• Smart reminders during your active hours
• Daily streaks with freeze protection
• Your last 7 days of posture history and trends
• Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets

POSTURE+
• Quiet all-day AirPods background coaching — a gentle nudge when you start to slouch, even with your phone in your pocket
• Apple Watch companion app with background coaching
• Your full posture history, hour by hour — see exactly when you drift
• Always-on monitoring — set it and forget it
• Priority support

WHICH AIRPODS WORK
Posture reads head motion from AirPods Pro (1st & 2nd gen), AirPods (3rd gen), AirPods 4 with Active Noise Cancellation, or AirPods Max. No AirPods to hand? You can still log check-ins by hand and keep your streak alive.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no sign-up
• No ads and no cross-app tracking of your activity
• Head-motion data is processed on-device and never leaves your phone
• AirPods and Apple Watch motion data stays on-device
• You can use the entire app without ever creating an account
