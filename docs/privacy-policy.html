Posture Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Posture is an AI posture coach for iPhone, AirPods, and Apple Watch. The core experience is free. An optional Posture Pro subscription unlocks premium features. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built.

Data Collection

Posture does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

  • No camera frames leave your device
  • No health or motion data is sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No third-party SDKs or frameworks (except Apple's StoreKit for purchases)
  • No advertising
  • No account creation required

Purchases & Subscriptions

Posture offers an optional Posture Pro subscription that unlocks premium features (AirPods monitoring, Apple Watch background coaching, always-on mode).

All payments are processed exclusively by Apple through StoreKit. Posture never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information — including credit card details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials.

To determine whether you have an active Posture Pro subscription, the app stores a subscription-status flag locally on your device. This flag is used solely to show or hide premium features and is never transmitted off your device.

Your purchase history and subscription management (upgrade, downgrade, cancel, or request a refund) are handled entirely by Apple through your Apple ID account. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Following a free trial or paid subscription period, if you cancel, your access to Posture Pro features ends at the close of the current billing period. No data is lost — your posture data and preferences remain on your device, and the free tier continues to work as before.

Camera Data

Posture requests access to your iPhone's front camera to measure head angle and forward lean during posture sessions. Camera frames are processed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework and are never stored, saved to disk, or transmitted anywhere. Each frame is analyzed in real time and immediately discarded.

You control when the camera is active by starting and stopping sessions. Posture only accesses the camera during an active session.

Motion Data

Posture Pro subscribers can enable AirPods head-motion monitoring via CMHeadphoneMotionManager. This data is processed entirely on-device to detect head angle changes and is never stored or transmitted.

HealthKit Data

Posture Pro subscribers can enable Apple Watch background monitoring via HKWorkoutSession. Posture requests read-only access to HealthKit to start a brief workout session for background wrist-motion detection. Posture does not write any data to Apple Health.

You can revoke Posture's HealthKit access at any time by going to Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Posture.

Local Storage

Posture stores your posture sessions, calibration data, streak state, and preferences locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is shared with the Apple Watch app via App Group. This data never leaves your device.

Your preferences (goals, calibration baseline, notification settings) are stored locally in UserDefaults on your device.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data remains on your device until you delete the app. Deleting Posture removes all locally stored data. No data persists on any external server because no data is ever transmitted externally.

To delete your data, you can either uninstall the app (which removes all stored data) or revoke camera, motion, and HealthKit access in Settings. No additional request is needed since no data exists outside your device.

Children's Privacy

Posture does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any information from any user.
