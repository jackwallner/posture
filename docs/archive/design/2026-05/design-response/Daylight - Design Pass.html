Posture · v0.1 design pass · for app store submission

Daylight.

A calm, paper-and-sage identity for a posture habit you can actually keep. Replaces the blue→purple gradient and SF-Symbol traffic light with a warm earth palette, a horizon metaphor for alignment, and a tonal three-step quality scale (aligned · drifting · resting) that never scolds.

Why serif italics? They give the wellness moments — your alignment score, the tip of the day, the result word — a hand-written, journal-like quality that visually separates them from system chrome. The system serif design ships with iOS — no licensed font, no shipped binary.

10

Decisions, briefly stated.

One-liner answers to the 9 in open-questions.md. Full reasoning lives in design-response.md.

1 · Streak prominence

Demote. Small numeric ("12 days") in nav toolbar. No flame icon on Today. Keep streak service intact for History journal entries and the year-end look-back.

2 · "Bad posture" framing

Tonal three-step: aligned · drifting · resting. Body copy avoids imperatives — "Curled forward. Take a slow breath." not "Straighten up."

3 · Pro affordances

Preview-with-lock for cohesion (the 24-hour rhythm block is visible on free with a tonal sage card overlaying it). Hide nothing — wellness apps lose trust when surfaces vanish.

4 · Calibration intensity

Stays utility. The full-bleed camera + oval guide is enough of a brand moment; don't invent a flow for a 5-second capture.

5 · Camera framing

Full-bleed during scan (Quick Scan + Calibration). Oval guide line. Quality chip floats on the preview. Inset rectangle is killed.

6 · Brand voice

Lowercase, observational, never imperative. "next nudge 3:00 pm" — not "Time to check your posture!" Reminder titles rotate from a single tone-of-voice spec in design-response.md.

7 · App icon

Flag for a follow-up pass. Current figure.stand motif is incompatible with the horizon metaphor; propose a horizon-and-sun icon for v1.0.

8 · Empty-state philosophy

Illustrate the future, never hide. Every empty surface gets a one-line italic invitation + the PostureBanner. No surface disappears.

9 · AirPods orange indicator

If Engineering keeps the feature: name it. Settings shows "Quiet AirPods background" with a one-line "iOS shows an orange dot — that's us, listening to nothing." If cut, no design owed.
