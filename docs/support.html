Posture is an AI posture coach for iPhone, AirPods, and Apple Watch that helps you build better posture habits with camera-based sessions and Duolingo-style streaks.

What Posture Needs

For the core free features, Posture requires:

  • Camera access — front camera to measure head angle during sessions

For Posture+ features (optional):

  • Motion access — AirPods head-motion data for passive monitoring
  • HealthKit access — Apple Watch background monitoring via workout sessions

Troubleshooting

  • If Posture shows no data, open Settings and confirm Posture has camera permission.
  • If calibration seems off, run a new calibration by sitting up straight and tapping Calibrate in Settings.
  • If AirPods monitoring isn't working, confirm motion permissions are granted in Settings and your AirPods are connected.
  • If Apple Watch nudges aren't coming through, check that HealthKit permissions are granted and your watch is on your wrist.
  • If streaks aren't updating, open the app and start a session to refresh the streak state.

Privacy

Posture does not create an account, does not send your data to a server, and does not include analytics, ads, or tracking. Camera frames are processed on-device and immediately discarded.
