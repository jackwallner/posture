How it's built and why it matters.

The Pitch

Most posture tools are one-time gadgets you forget about. I wanted something that lives on the devices you already carry — your iPhone, AirPods, and Apple Watch — and gently coaches you throughout the day without being annoying.

Posture turns the AirPods you already own into an always-on posture coach. Pop them in and Posture quietly watches your head position all day, giving you a gentle nudge the moment you settle into a real slouch. Your phone builds a daily report from how you actually sat, not spot checks.

Posture+

Posture+ is a subscription with monthly and yearly plans plus a one-time lifetime unlock. A free trial is available on the subscription plans. $4.99/month, $29.99/year, or $79.99 lifetime.

AirPods Monitoring. All-day background head-motion monitoring via CMHeadphoneMotionManager. Get gentle nudges when your head angle drops below your calibrated baseline.

Apple Watch Integration. Background coaching on your wrist via the companion watch app.

Daily Reports & Streaks. Minute-by-minute posture aggregates, weekly trends, and Duolingo-style streaks with freeze protection.

Manage or cancel any time in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

What Mattered

Zero-config setup. Open the app, do a quick calibration sitting up straight, and you're ready to start. No account, no onboarding flow, no tutorial.

Multi-device coaching. AirPods for the core read, iPhone for reports and history, Apple Watch for wrist coaching. Each device plays a role that fits how you use it.

Streaks that stick. Duolingo-style streaks with freeze protection keep you motivated without punishing off days. The flame grows as your streak builds.

Privacy as a feature. No analytics, no accounts, no data leaving your device. Your AirPods' head-motion data is processed on-device and never recorded or transmitted.
