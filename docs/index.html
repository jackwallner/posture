How it's built and why it matters.

The Pitch

Most posture tools are one-time gadgets you forget about. I wanted something that lives on the devices you already carry — your iPhone, AirPods, and Apple Watch — and gently coaches you throughout the day without being annoying.

Posture uses your iPhone camera for deliberate posture sessions, AirPods for passive head-motion monitoring, and Apple Watch for background nudges. Three devices, one goal: better posture without thinking about it.

Posture+

An optional upgrade for always-on coaching. $4.99/month, $29.99/year, or $79.99 lifetime, with a 7-day free trial.

AirPods Monitoring. Your AirPods detect head motion via CMHeadphoneMotionManager. Get gentle nudges when your head angle drops below your calibrated baseline.

Apple Watch Integration. Background monitoring via HKWorkoutSession on your wrist. Your watch taps you when it's time to straighten up — no phone needed.

Always-On Mode. Posture+ runs quietly in the background. No need to open the app or start a session. Just go about your day and get nudges when your posture slips.

All Free Features. Camera sessions, Duolingo-style streaks, calibration, live scoring, and history are free forever.

What Mattered

Zero-config setup. Open the app, do a quick calibration sitting up straight, and you're ready to start. No account, no onboarding flow, no tutorial.

Multi-device coaching. iPhone for deliberate sessions, AirPods for passive monitoring, Apple Watch for background nudges. Each device plays a role that fits how you use it.

Streaks that stick. Duolingo-style streaks with freeze protection keep you motivated without punishing off days. The flame grows as your streak builds.

Privacy as a feature. No analytics, no accounts, no data leaving your device. Camera frames are processed with on-device Vision and discarded immediately.
